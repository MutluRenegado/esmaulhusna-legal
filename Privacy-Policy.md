PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: March 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Esmaül Hüsna mobile application ("the App") handles information when you use the application.

1. Overview

The App is designed to help users learn and reflect on the 99 Names of Allah. The App respects user privacy and does not collect personally identifiable information.

2. Information Collected

The App does not collect personal data such as:

• Name
• Email address
• Location data
• Contact information
• Account credentials

3. Local Device Storage

The App may store certain preferences locally on your device, including:

• Theme preferences
• App settings
• Favorite names or bookmarks
• Audio playback preferences

This information remains stored only on the user's device and is not transmitted to any external servers.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not use advertising networks or third-party tracking services.

If future versions integrate external services, the Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for users of all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Data Security

Because the App does not collect personal data, there is minimal risk related to data storage or misuse.

7. External Links

The App may include links to external websites. The developer is not responsible for the privacy practices of those third-party sites.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Updates will be reflected within the App.

9. Contact

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer.
